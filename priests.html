    What are Stocks?



    When we talk about trading stocks, we usually are talking about
     "shares" which just means that each "share" shows that you own
    a small piece of a company (for example, if you were to buy one
     share of AAPL (apple), you would own about one billionth of the
     company ($542 per share).

    Short VS Long



    Going "long" a stock just means that you have purchased shares and own them.

    Going "short" is completely opposite. Instead of making money when the stock goes up and losing money when it goes down, when you short stock you make money when the stock goes down and lose money when the stock goes up.
