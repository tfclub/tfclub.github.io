Trading and Finance Club (TFC) - Luke Aslanian



Open either a TradeMonster PaperTrade or thinkorswim papermoney account. You can Google either term or go to either of the following links:

 

TradeMonster PaperTrade

 

or

 

thinkorswim paperMoney

 

Anyone can participate but must not reset their accounts nor cheat the amount of money. Both things are possible; but I have devised a way that I'll know if you do. On TradeMonster, you'll all make a trade before a certain time so I know you didn't reset your accounts, and on thinkorswim I'll base win/loss on profit/loss and also look on each account, as I can tell if you changed the amount of money or not.
Everyone must also give me their username and password. Don't use this password on other sites, and this shouldn't be a problem. Only I will see these passwords. Also, You may not make a trade in the first 15 nor last 15 minutes of each trading day. This should get rid of most ways of cheating. I'll be able to see if you guys didn't follow one of these things, and each wrongful trade's profit will be taken away if wrongfully made, or you could be disqualified.

The Prizes:

1st Place- $100 (minimum; possibly more) and your name on a plaque.
2nd Place- $50 (again, see later for more info on possibly diff. or greater prizes)
3rd Place- $25
Bonus- If you beat my TradeMonster account (which I haven't actively traded for a pretty long time but still turned from the default 100k into around 230K) you will receive at least a $100 bonus (only one person will, the highest account balance above my current one at the end of the contest)!

These are personally paid by Luke. If any parent or someone else wants to raise the prizes, just talk to me and we'll make it happen. I'll probably be talking to a few financial industry people I know, but it would be awesome if someone would decide to do that, again- just let me know. Thanks!

I'll post each persons returns on the website (http://errorrat.github.io/contest.html) so you guys can know where you stand and compete with everyone. Derek, don't get too obsessed.
